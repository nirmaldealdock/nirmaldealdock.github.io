Revolutionizing Deal Management & Modeling

Who are we?

Smart, simple solutions are at the core of all that we do at DealDock. We are modernizing the deal flow process by integrating the automation of end-to-end deal flow and financial modeling with a seamless user experience, specifically in private equity. We believe that this will rapidly increase the efficiency for our clients.

Our Product

Efficient Deal Tracking

We offer end-to-end deal tracking from the moment that one receives a potential deal in their inbox to when the deal is finally closed. Centralization and organization of large quantities of key information in the digital age is critical to business functions.

Info Processing

Analysts have spent far too long on manually extracting information through long CIMs and deal teasers. It's time to automate this process and save crucial time that could be better spent elsewhere. Our engineers will use state of the art natural language processing to ensure top-notch accuracy.

Assisted Modeling

Our goal is to harness the power of machine learning and computation when developing financial models such as LBOs. We will use a large stream of quantitative information to improve and curate our suggestions to your firm regarding specific deals.
